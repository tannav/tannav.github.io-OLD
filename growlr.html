Growlr App Proposal

Oregon State University Interactive Design

This was a group project that I completed during my time at Oregon State University. We were tasked with thinking of an app that would solve a need for college students, and then to create a front-end UI for it.

The Proposal

The concept that my group came up with was an app that allowed users to easily find local happy hours, and then sync the app with their friends and then be notified if their friends were planning on going to a happy hour and invite other friends.

The Outcome

As part of the group, a classmate and I worked out UX wireframes, after which I implemented our concept into a full blown User Interface.
