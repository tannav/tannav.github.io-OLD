2017 Email Series

eROI Internal Emails

During my time at the Portland based Digital Agency eROI, I was placed in charge of the design direction and implementation of our emails. Going into 2017, we moved away from our previous template and wanted to create a new system and design aesthetic that could be used for an entire year.

The Proposal

The direction I proposed looked at our agency values and how we challenged each other daily to be better humans, designers and citizens. Through collaboration with the strategist and content specialist assigned to the project, the theme we came up with for 2017 was "Dare to Be" – where we would challenge our readers to make a positive change in their life.

The Outcome

From a visual perspective, I created illustrations of 7 characters and began a design system that I modified and improved upon based upon feedback. I designed the first three emails in collaboration with our development team, after which another designer took over the series based on a pre-planned designer change.
