Typographic Map:
United States of America

Personal Project

This is a self guided project that I completed over the course of a summer in which I handlettered and then vectorized all 50 states in the US with their state nickname. 

The Proposal

This project began with a state of Oregon with the text "The Beaver State" that I made for a friend as a birthday present, however as I was working on it and enjoyed the process I decided to continue making states.

The Outcome

The final product for the project was a map of the United States, where each state was done in a similar style with variance throughout to ensure dynamism and make the map less repetitive.
